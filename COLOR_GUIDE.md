### Navy Blue
- **Associations**: Trust, stability, professionalism, calm
- **Use Case**: Perfect for a productivity app, conveys reliability
- **Effect**: Helps users feel focused and organized

### Beige
- **Associations**: Warmth, comfort, natural, neutral
- **Use Case**: Reduces eye strain, comfortable for long sessions
- **Effect**: Creates a welcoming, stress-free environment
